Our Story

Hari Narayan Gupta

Founder & Chief Cinematographer, HP Films

With over two and a half decades of dedicated craft behind the lens, Hari Narayan Gupta has built HP Films into one of Kanpur's most trusted names in wedding cinematography and photography. Since establishing the studio in 1998, his singular vision has remained unchanged — to document love stories with honesty, artistry, and lasting grace.

Hari's approach is rooted in observation. Rather than directing moments, he captures them as they unfold — the quiet glance before the vows, the laughter that breaks through tears, the stillness between celebrations. Every frame is composed with purpose, every film edited with narrative care.

Over the years, HP Films has had the privilege of telling hundreds of unique love stories across Kanpur and beyond, earning the trust of families who value authenticity over spectacle.
